Who We Are

Welcome to Kabod City Church

We are a Pentecostal church rooted in the heart of Moi's Bridge Town, Kenya, committed to glorifying God and building a community grounded in faith, love, and the power of the Holy Spirit.

We are dedicated to equipping saints through The Word, Prayer, and Fellowship — believing that every believer is called to grow, serve, and impact their world for the Kingdom of God.

Under the leadership of Pastor Lenny Wafula Kinisu, we welcome all to come and experience the presence of God with us.

📖
The Word

Grounded in Scripture, we teach and preach the undiluted Word of God to transform lives and build strong believers.

🙏
Prayer

We are a house of prayer — believing in the power of intercession, worship, and intimate communion with God.

🤝
Fellowship

We build a family atmosphere where believers grow together, support one another, and walk in unity and love.

The Word

Sermons & Messages

We believe in the power of God's Word to transform, heal, and deliver. Join us every service as Pastor Lenny Wafula Kinisu ministers the living Word of God. Below are sermon highlights from our recent services.

Isaiah 60:1
Arise and Shine — The Glory of the Lord

A powerful message on walking in the fullness of God's glory and living above every darkness in this season.

Acts 1:8
Power From On High

Understanding the role of the Holy Spirit in the life of a believer and how to receive and operate in His power daily.

Ephesians 4:12
Equipped for the Work

Discovering your calling and being equipped as a saint to fulfill God's purpose in your family, community, and nation.
